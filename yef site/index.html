Together for an East African Future

Youth-led action for unity, empowerment, and regional development.

Youth for East African Federation (YEF) is a grassroots, youth-led movement championing a united, prosperous East Africa.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

A vibrant East Africa where young people work across borders to create shared prosperity, peace, and opportunity.

Mission

Equip and mobilize youth to build practical projects that demonstrate the value of a federated East Africa.

What We Do

Unity

We foster cross-border exchange, cultural understanding, and shared identity through dialogue and exchange programs.

Empowerment

We train young leaders in civic engagement, entrepreneurship, and digital skills to create jobs and drive inclusion.

Development

We champion projects and policies that improve mobility, trade, and innovation across the region.
